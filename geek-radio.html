About the Station

G.E.E.K. Radio 305 is the flagship audio broadcast of the IIIGLOBE network — a curated soundscape for the gifted, the educated, the empowered. Broadcasting culture, conversation, and coded frequency out of Miami, FL.

Expect deep dives into tech, innovation, Hip-Hop culture, entrepreneurship, and the stories that mainstream radio won't tell. Every episode is a transmission built to elevate.
